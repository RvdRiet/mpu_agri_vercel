POPIA Compliance

The Mpumalanga Livestock Development Programme and the Department of Agriculture, Rural Development Land and Environmental Affairs are committed to compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA).

How we use your personal information

Personal information collected through this website and application process (including but not limited to your name, South African ID number, contact details, address, and information relating to your farming operation) is used solely for the purpose of administering the Masibuyele Esibayeni Programme, assessing applications, and communicating with you regarding your application.

Lawful processing

We process your personal information in accordance with POPIA on the basis of:

  • Your consent, where you provide information voluntarily in your application;
  • Performance of a contract or steps prior to entering into a contract (programme participation);
  • Compliance with a legal obligation; and
  • Legitimate interests of the Department in delivering the programme.

Your rights

In terms of POPIA you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you;
  • Request correction of inaccurate or outdated information;
  • Object to the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances;
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator.

Security and retention

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss or misuse. Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.

Contact

For any queries regarding the processing of your personal information or to exercise your rights, please contact the Department using the contact details provided on the programme information page.
