Definition of farmer category

Subsistence farmer — practices agriculture mainly for household food security. Not classified as indigent. Annual turnover up to R50,000.

Smallholder farmer — produces for household consumption and markets. Annual turnover R50,001 to R1,000,000.

Commercial producer — established farming venture for production and sale of agricultural products. Farm income exceeds R1,000,000 per annum.
