Livestock Management Programme

The Masibuyele Esibayeni Programme supports livestock farmers across Mpumalanga with breeding stock allocation for beef cattle, dairy, goats, sheep, pigs, poultry, and rabbits. Grow your herd with government-backed grants tailored to your farmer category.

Programme Overview

Masibuyele Esibayeni Programme

The Masibuyele Esibayeni Programme (meaning “Let us return to livestock”) is a provincial initiative by the Mpumalanga Department of Agriculture to revitalise livestock farming. It provides qualifying farmers with breeding stock — bulls, cows, rams, ewes, bucks, does, boars, sows, and poultry — based on their farmer category and annual turnover. The programme aims to build sustainable herds, improve food security, and support economic development in rural areas.

Farmer categories are determined by annual agricultural turnover: Subsistence (up to R50,000), Smallholder (R50,001 – R1,000,000), and Commercial (exceeds R1,000,000). Allocations for commercial farmers are subject to individual assessment.

Commercial Farmers (exceeds R1,000,000)

Subject to individual assessment. Commercial farmers with annual agricultural turnover exceeding R1,000,000 are assessed on a case-by-case basis. Contact the Department of Agriculture for eligibility and allocation details.

Eligibility

Application Requirements

To apply for livestock support under the Masibuyele Esibayeni Programme, you must meet the following eligibility requirements:

  • Valid South African ID and residency in Mpumalanga province, or proof of farming within Mpumalanga
  • Proof of land access — lease, PTO (Permission to Occupy), RTO (Right to Occupy), or title deed
  • Registration as a farmer or proof of farming activity
  • Business plan or business profile outlining your livestock enterprise
  • Commitment to participate in extension services and animal health programmes
  • Not previously disqualified from departmental livestock programmes
  • Appropriate infrastructure and capacity to care for allocated animals (where applicable)

Beyond the Grant

Additional Support & Financing

Besides the Masibuyele Esibayeni grant, these provincial programmes can support your livestock operation.

MADC Loans

The Mpumalanga Agricultural Development Corporation offers seasonal loans for inputs and business loans for farm improvements such as fencing, kraals, and equipment.
